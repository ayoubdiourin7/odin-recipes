Margherita

Description

Margherita pizza is a pizza prepared according to a recipe of the Italian chef Raffaelle Esposito. It is made with tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, salt and extra-virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated pizza crust dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni sausage
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a pizza pan or medium baking sheet.
  2. Press pizza dough into pan or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a 1 inch border. Sprinkle mozzarella, Cheddar and Parmesan cheeses over sauce. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms, green pepper, onion and olives.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve hot.
